Intro

About Me

Hi, I'm Dan. I'm a 1st year student at Staffordshire University currently studying Computer Games Programming.

I'm currently studying about the fundamentals of game creation and what it takes to build yourself in the gaming industry. I am a very dedicated individual who strives to ensure that tasks are completed on time and to a high standard. I am highly adaptable and wish to put as much effort and detail into my projects as I can.

My Qualifications

I am currently working my way towards a degree in Computer Games Programming at Staffordshire University.

I have A-Levels in Computer Science and a BTEC Level 3 qualification in Business Studies.
